Hey dear BeatSaber community :heart: I would like to present you a new way to manage your BeatSaber custom songs quickly and easily.\ This project was launched with the intention of simplifying song `downloading` and `management`.\ There are already more ideas for this and I'm also happy to get some `inspiration` from `YOU`.
  • BeatSaver inspired design
  • Browse custom songs from BeatSaver
  • Download/Install songs
  • Browse installed songs
  • Delete installed sogs
  • Show personal song stats
  • Download queue
  • Mappers pages
  • Playlist manager
